Creating a virtual machine using oracle virtual box.

Step 1: Open oracle virtualbox and click on New.

Step 2: After clicking on new, give name, folder pathlocation
and iso file then click on hardware.

Step 3: after that click on hardware and give base memory to
8gb and 4 processors.

Step 4 : After that click on Hard Disk and allocate to 30gb and
then click on Finish.

Step 5: click on start.

Step 6: u will get on this screen and start the process by clicking
Next.

Step 7: click on Install now

Step 8: after completing the installation, and create a account
then u will see this page.
